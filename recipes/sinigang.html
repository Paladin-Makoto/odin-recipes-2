High Speed Sinigang

Savory Pork and Vegetable in sour tamarind soup, done fast.

Ingredients

  • Pork, Country Style Cuts
  • Onions
  • Okra
  • Gabi
  • Tomatoes
  • Kang kong
  • Water
  • Sitaw
  • Tamarind Soup Mix
  • Pork Boullion Broth

Directions

  1. Start Searing Pork in wok or pot
  2. While Searing, Roughly Chop Vegetables
  3. Add Water to pork, extract fond
  4. Add Tomatoes, Onions and Gabi
  5. Bring to a boil
  6. Add Boullion and Mixes, lower heat
  7. Cook until cooking time is 20 mins
  8. Add remaining Vegetables
  9. Let simmer for 10 more mins
  10. Serve
